About Our Services

Each service includes access to our professional specialists and state of the art facility and equipment to help you get back to your desired physical level.

This service includes range of motion exercise, progressive resistive exercise, balance training, strength training, and aerobic conditioning.

This service includes kneading and manipulation of muscles, joint mobilization, and joint manipulation to treat your muscle, ligament and bone needs.

This service aims to stimulate the body's natural healing abilities and promote physical and emotional well-being by the insertion of very thin needles into certain parts on your body.

This service aims to reduce pain and improve function by transmitting electrical, sound, light, or thermal energy into the body.

This service aims to relieve your jaw pain by practicing jaw exercises, heat therapy, ice therapy, massages, and training to improve posture and jaw alignment.
